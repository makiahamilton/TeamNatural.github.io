Products

4A
4B
4C
  • A gel product will be your best friend when aiming for curl definition and when styling your wash and gos.
  • Thicker creams and butters like shea butter are best for maximizing all Type 4 wash and gos, twist outs, etc.
  • Creme of Natural Argan Oil is a perfect edge control for all hair types
  • The LCO (liquid, cream, oil) or LOC (liquid, oil, cream) methods to moisturize your curls

  • This tip also goes for all hair types, using a microfiber towel or a tshirt to dry your hair after washing as terry cloth strips more moisture and causes more tension and tears on your hair strands

  • Trader Joe's brand Coconut Oil
  • Argan Oil Eco Styler Gel
