Styling Tips

3A
3B
3C

Your favorite style is most likely the wash and go look. An easy easy way to achieve this is to follow your every day wash routine, apply the recommended products and then use a ceramic blow dryer with a diffuser attachment. You can do so many things with your hair. Pin it up on both sides or just one, and if all else fails pull it back into one big curly pony tail.

You would most likely want to follow the same steps as 3A the only difference is depending on the volume and denstiy of your hair you may want to use a slightly heavier product to ensure a greater hold. Use the "pineapple" method while drying your hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz.

An easy fix is a Wash and go using sulfate, paraben, and silicone free products. Co-wash at least twice a week. The absolute key for types 3C and up is to deep condition frequently! It will save the life of your culrs by replenishing all the of the lost moisture. The pineapple method can also be a life saver for 3C hair. Since lack of volume is not necessarily your biggest problem you can stand to use a heavier product for a better hold.

Do's

3A
3B
3C

Deep condition frequently! Trim ends every 3 or so months

Use prodcts with a lighter hold. Use nutrient bassed products rather than products that contain excessive amounts of alcohol. Seal in moisture with leave-in oil on wash day.

Use a spray bottle and moisturizer to revitalize your curls. Pineapple. Deep conditioning is essential. Sleep with a satin pillow case or bonnet. Finger detangle, use wide toothed comb, or denman brush.

Dont's

3A
3B
3C

Co-washing may weigh your hair down. using heavy styling creams will also work against achieving volume.

Don't use products with heavy sulfates and alcohols. You don't want to use heavy oils either. Find a happy medium between the two.

Do not use too much heat, as tempting as it may be, limit the amount you process your hair with heat. Dont brush dry curls or use a brissel brush to detangle this will result in breakage and unmanageable frizz.
